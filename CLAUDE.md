## Website Structure (Planned)

| Page | Hebrew | Content |
|------|--------|---------|
| Home | עמוד בית | Mission, intro, call-to-action |
| About | מי אנחנו | Story, values, team |
| Activities | מה אנחנו עושים | Programs, services, locations |
| Stories | סיפורים והשפעה | Impact stories, testimonials |
| Donate | תרומה ותמיכה | Donation options (Bit, bank transfer) |
| Contact | יצירת קשר | Contact info, volunteer info |

## Key Questionnaire Sections

1. **Brand basics** - Colors, tone, target audience
2. **Home page** - Opening statement, founder story, values
3. **Activities** - Programs, locations, numbers
4. **Stories** - Impact stories, quotes
5. **Donations** - Goals, amounts, channels
6. **Contact** - Team, communication channels, volunteers

## Target Audiences

- Donors (private and institutional)
- Soldiers and families needing support
- Volunteers
- Government/authorities

## Bilingual Considerations

- Primary: Hebrew (RTL)
